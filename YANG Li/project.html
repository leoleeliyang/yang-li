Mandarin Learning Mobile App "SAY IT"

Overview

A Mandarin learning app you will never get tired of it！

Problem

In my literature review, I examed 17 market-available Chinese spoken(Mandarin)learning apps.It is shown through my review that the current apps for spoken Chinese(Mandarin) learning fails to improve the speaking skills of Chinese learners adequately. After interviewing some mandarin learners,there are five problems that I found from current Mandarin learning app.

First, Regarding content, with materials only concentrating on daily-life conversations, the need for diversified topics is neglected which is challenging for learners with different targets to meet their needs.Besides that, the learning content that apps provide is very outdated; some apps even never update their learning content since put into market and neglect what the people currently speak in the real-life situation.

Secondly, in term of the feedback system, most apps give the learners feedback based on AI. It can not deny that AI is the low-cost way to give relatively accurate feedback but compared to the online teacher, AI can only tell learns what the pronunciation mistake is but will never tell learners why they are wrong and how to fix it.

Thirdly, concerning function, most apps employ the method of human-computer interaction for practicing, which lacks human-to-human interaction and language use in a real-life situation. Learning app is not only for learning, but it also should provide a function that let user practice what they learned.

Next,for the practice mode, although the utterly mechanical way of imitation can improve the accuracy of learner’s pronunciation, long-term practice will do great harm to learner’s enthusiasm, and the pleasure of active learning will disappear as well.

Last, Regarding the platform and charge, most apps should be paid, and only a few of them provide the service on both IOS and Android platforms.

User scenarios

[problem scenario] [Activity scenario] [Information scenario] [interaction scenario]

James is a 15-year-old American high school boy who is very interested in Chinese. He took the Chinese course in the school and found that the pronunciation part of Chinese was very difficult for him. After the high school Chinese course, he hopes to continue to train his pronunciation. He has a crush on a Chinese girl, and he hopes to express his love to that girl through WeChat in standard mandarin. But when he put out his voice, the girl did not understand him at all, he was very frustrated.

He sought help from his friends who recommended him an app for learning spoken Chinese. With the app, there are rich conversations and sentences about daily life in Chinese. He found the chapter about love, which is like how to express love in standard mandarin.

The app provides a real conversation scenario in which a boy is expressing his love for a girl. It allows learners to conduct listening training first, and then divide the conversation into parts so that learners can repeat the sentences with the recoding on- bye-one. After repeating, learners will be given marks and feedback immediately and informed of which part they need to correct. Learners can repeat one sentence until the system prompts for the next one. After learning, the learner is asked to dub the dialogue.

After dubbing, he can click on the function of “online partners ”and “online teachers”, connecting with people who are using the app in the world and correcting his pronunciation by chatting with real people for improving his ability to use the language in real context through interaction.

Wireframing & Prototyping

With the proper planning, we were able to confidently move into creating wireframes for the app. We took the decision to focus more on the functionality and structure of the app, opting for low-fidelity wireframes with very little detail. Ultimately, we could add design elements in later, what was important for our users, was that the product (above all) was clear and simple.

  2. One on one teacher courses

    Design

    For our question above, we have designed our app. Our app offers two different types of courses, topic courses, and one-on-one teacher courses. In the topic course, we offer a wide range of courses for learners to choose from. More importantly, our course content follows current hot topics, and students can learn the most authentic Chinese spoken expression.In the one-to-one teacher course, students can choose their favorite teachers to conduct targeted training according to their own interests, so that students' learning is more personalized.In the topic course, after learning the course content, students can make an appointment with the online teacher according to their needs. The course is free and can help students correct their pronunciation.Students not only know where they are wrong but also know why they are wrong and how to correct them.In order to improve students' ability to use language, we designed a live broadcast platform and a personal vlog uploading platform. Through personal live broadcast, students can apply the learned knowledge and truly apply what they have learned.Personal live broadcasts and vlog videos are currently the most popular social activities. These two function are also designed to motivate students to learn and use our app as much as possible

    Figure 6. SAY IT's final iOS application design.

    User feedback

    Our app draft evaluation went very smoothly, and the process of talk-aloud is detailed in the design document. Our high-fidelity design has had two user tests, and the biggest problem users think is that it is impossible to distinguish between the appointment of a topic course and the appointment of a one-on-one teacher. Many users can no longer find their own reservation information after booking a course. According to the user's opinion, we have designed a new calendar function in the menu bar of the homepage and separated the appointment color of the topic course from the one-on-one course color. thus，students can see at a glance which course they have booked on any given day. On the student's personal information page, we also set up a new appointment information viewing function, from the perspective of whether the student completed the appointment of the course so that students can know which appointment courses they have already taken, and which scheduled courses have not been conducted.
